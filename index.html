About Us

Welcome to Blue Eagle Junk Removal, your premier junk removal company in Oakland County, MI. We provide a variety of hauling services, including garage cleanup, hoarding cleanup, yard cleanup, post-construction cleanup, hot tub removal, and debris removal services. We have the skill and experience necessary to serve as your full-service professional cleaners, offering the highest quality services and customer care in the Highland area. Blue Eagle Junk Removal offers respect, dedication, integrity, and a commitment to exceeding our customers' expectations. We specialize in commercial junk removal and residential junk removal services. Our mission is to make sure that we are consistently offering the highest quality clean up service. We strive to deliver complete customer satisfaction for every cleanup service we provide, affordable pricing, and excellent customer service. At Blue Eagle Junk Removal, we make every effort to maintain trusting relationships with our clients, not merely to offer junk removal services, but to be a part of your routine for a better, cleaner, healthier life. If you are interested in acquiring our clean up services or if you would like to learn more about what we can do for you, go ahead and contact us at Blue Eagle Junk Removal today for more information.
